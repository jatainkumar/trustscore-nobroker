1. Problem Statement

The Indian rental market suffers from a massive information asymmetry. Landlords demand 6-10 months of deposit because they cannot verify a tenant's reliability. Traditional CIBIL scores do not track rental behavior.

2. The AI Solution

We trained a Linear Regression model (see Simulator above) that ingests:

  • Payment History: Data from NoBroker Pay transactions.
  • Digital Footprint: Utility bill payments via Account Aggregator.
  • Social Signals: Employment verification via LinkedIn.
